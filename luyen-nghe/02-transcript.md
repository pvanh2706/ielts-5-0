Hello. This is a message for Mr. Nam.

My name is Linda Wilson. That's W-I-L-S-O-N.

I'm calling from the City Dental Clinic, about your appointment with the dentist.

You asked to see the dentist on Tuesday. But I'm very sorry — the doctor is not free on Tuesday. So your appointment is now on Thursday, at half past two in the afternoon.

The clinic is at number forty, Green Street. That's four — zero, Green Street. It is near the big market.

The check-up costs thirty-five dollars and fifty cents.

If you cannot come on Thursday, please call me back. My number is: oh, nine, one, seven — double three, five — five, eight, two.

Thank you. Goodbye.
